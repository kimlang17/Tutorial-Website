Tips Productive for UX/UI design?

1. Use a tool like Asana, Trello, or Jira to track your tasks and projects with your team and clients. This will be very helpful for you to stay organized and avoid overlap in tasks.

2. Use a design tool like Sketch, Adobe XD, or Figma which is run smoothly in your device to create your designs. Remember bro, if you can work without any stuck, then your project is complete so soon.

3. Use a browser extension like Momentum, StayFocusd, or RescueTime to help you stay focused while you are working. And sometimes you may go outside or talk with your friends and family.

4. Use noise-canceling headphones to help you focus on your work.

5. Use a standing desk or a treadmill desk to help you stay active while working for avoiding back pain.

6. Use a Pomodoro timer(software, app or google chrome extension) to help you break up your work into smaller chunks.

7. Use a time tracking tool like RescueTime or others to help you track how much time youâ€™re spending on each task.

8. Use a team chat tool like Slack or Google Hangouts or skype to communicate with your team. This will help you to stay organized and avoid overlap in communication with your team member.

9. Walk or do simple exercise and drink water. This is very important to your health. If you are not fit well, then your productivity ratio reduces day by day.

10. At last talk with other UI/UX designer, this will keep you motivated and encouraged you to make great UI/UX design work.
